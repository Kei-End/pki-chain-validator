Enhanced Certificate Chain Validator

This build accepts PEM, CRT, CER, and DER certificate files, reconstructs the chain, validates the trust path, checks basic ECC policy, warns on likely family mismatch such as GlobalSign R5 versus E46, and suggests the likely bundle correction when a wrong root or intermediate is supplied.

Input

You may upload one or more certificate files. DER files are converted locally in the browser.
This page is fully client-side. Nothing is sent to a server. For best results, provide the full chain.
